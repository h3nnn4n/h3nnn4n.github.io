Fracture

October 13, 2018
javascript visual creative

Previously I worked on cracks, which was a reinterpretation of fractures, by inconvergent.net. My goal originally was to eventually reproduce his work, as an exercise. I stopped halfway there with something that I was satisfied with even though I will want to reproduce it somewhere in the future. From the partially finished reimplementation, I had the idea of using a picture to get the colors to fill in the spaces. The intention was to recreate a picture using a mosaic-like pattern generated by the cracks/fractures. Unfortunately, I was not able to do it fast enough using JavaScript. The partial result can be seen below. One can click the canvas to restart the drawing process and generate new images.
